Step #2  – nsKnox Application

Please note that AM Dofasco has partnered with a Third Party company, nsKnox (located in Tel-Aviv, Israel), for the validation of your bank information to prevent financial loss due to cybercrime.  This is for the security of both your company and AM Dofasco. nsKnox has been cc’d on this email.  You may contact nsKnox directly if you have any questions, and your company will be contacted by an nsKnox representative within 3 business days upon receipt of this email.

  1. To initiate the banking validation process please click on the following link: nsKnox Validation.
    • Instructions are located within the website listed above
    • Please note that for the validation with nsKnox, your company MUST use the same banking account details where AM Dofasco will be remitting your payments (i.e. the bank letter or void cheque submitted in Step #1)
      • For example, if AM Dofasco will be depositing into a Canadian bank account (CAD), your company must validate the Canadian bank account with nsKnox
        • This means a credit card cannot be used for validation, or a different bank account
  1. Your company will be requested to transact a nominal payment (~$10-$15 + applicable banking fees) for the validation to occur.
    • Any concerns regarding the fees associated with the validation can be discussed with dofasco@arcelormittal.com (cc’d on this email)
